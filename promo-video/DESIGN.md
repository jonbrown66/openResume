## Style Prompt

Create a calm, production-grade product promo for a Chinese resume editor. The visual identity must mirror the app shell: warm neutral canvas, soft green accent, frosted surfaces, compact professional controls, and a split editor/preview workspace. Motion should feel smooth, efficient, and product-led, with directional transitions that suggest moving through a real workflow.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#eef3e7`
- Shell: `#fbfcf8`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Ink: `#1b1f16`
- Muted text: `#687060`
- Border: `#dfe7d5`
- Accent: `#a9d56b`
- Accent hover: `#9ac95b`
- Dark ink: `#11150e`

## Typography

- UI and body: `Geist Variable`, `Arial`, sans-serif
- Resume preview: `Noto Serif SC`, `Georgia`, serif
- Data/code snippets: `JetBrains Mono`, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon, purple-blue gradients, or generic SaaS dark dashboards.
- Do not create floating marketing cards unrelated to the actual product UI.
- Do not use jump cuts between scenes.
- Do not use oversized explanatory paragraphs; keep copy readable at video scale.
- Do not introduce colors that are not traceable to the product palette.
